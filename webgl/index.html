Source parameters

Orientation of source relative to x-axis
Controls whether source is circular (q=1) or elliptical (q=0)
Higher values cause source brightness to decrease sharply with radius
Sets the size of the source

Lens parameters

Orientation of lens relative to x-axis
Controls whether lens is circular (q=1) or elliptical (q=0)
Sets the size of the lens

Telescope

Telescope noise level
